Compact Campus Board

The climbing gym my brother goes to near his college has no campus board. A campus board is a tool rock climbers use to exersize climbing without legs ("campusing"). All examples I've seen look very simple, so I thought it'd be a good Christmas gift to make one that you could easily swap out holds on and would fit in my brother's apartment.
